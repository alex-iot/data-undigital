About us

A specialist consultancy for complex digital innovation programmes

Data Undigital Ltd is a UK-registered advisory practice working across data infrastructure, IoT, connected systems and collaborative R&D. We help organisations shape technically credible programmes and create the conditions for them to be funded, adopted and delivered.

Our experience spans research labs, national innovation programmes, industrial clients, European R&D and venture development. Our advisory work is informed by first-hand founder experience: building a data platform company, developing a real-time urban data exchange into a SaaS proposition, raising early-stage funding, and navigating the realities of pilots, partnerships and commercial adoption.

We combine technical architecture, strategic programme design and stakeholder engagement — allowing us to contribute not only to the technology, but also to the partnerships, governance and delivery models around it.
